How Does It Work?

1. Understand the Challenge

Select a prompt from our dedicated tracks: Policy Making, Public Administration, or Smart Cities. Each prompt will provide a basic overview, but you are encouraged to dive deeper, research the issue, and explore how AI can revolutionize the public sector

2. Ideate & Sketch AI-Driven Solutions

Once you've grasped the challenge, start brainstorming. Create flowcharts, draft wireframes, and sketch out your concepts. Use methods like affinity mapping or mind mapping to refine your ideas, focusing on AI-driven solutions that can make a real impact in governance.

3. Transform Your Ideas into Digital Prototypes

With your sketches ready, it's time to bring them to life. Utilize tools like Figma, Adobe XD, or InVision Studio to convert your drafts into high-fidelity, interactive prototypes that showcase how AI can transform governance.

4. Submit & Influence the Future

Submit your AI-powered prototype to our expert panel of judges. The best designs not only win exciting prizes but also have the potential to influence the future of governance!
